# The Still app 90-day TikTok carousel content plan

A meditation app launching educational TikTok carousels faces one core challenge: teaching a progressive skill while keeping each post independently valuable and engaging. This plan solves that by structuring **90 daily carousel posts** across four phases — Foundation, Building Practice, Deepening, and Advanced Integration — rotating through seven content pillars that blend technique tutorials, science, myth-busting, and challenges. Every post is designed around a purple meditation character mascot, optimized for TikTok's carousel algorithm (which currently gives carousels **81% higher engagement** than video posts), and sequenced so a follower who discovers the account on Day 1 or Day 47 still gets immediate value.

The plan draws on curricula from Headspace, Calm, Waking Up, and MBSR, integrates breathwork progression validated by peer-reviewed research, and applies 2025–2026 TikTok carousel best practices. Below is the complete framework, carousel design system, and all 90 days mapped out.

---

## The four-phase progression framework

The 90 days break into four distinct phases, each with a clear purpose, technique set, and emotional arc that mirrors how real beginners actually experience meditation.

**Phase 1: Foundation (Days 1–21)** — "You can do this." The goal is habit formation and demystification. Techniques stay simple: breath awareness, body scan, diaphragmatic breathing, coherent breathing, and box breathing. Session recommendations start at **3–5 minutes**. Content tone is warm, reassuring, and myth-busting. The purple mascot appears as a friendly, slightly confused beginner alongside the audience.

**Phase 2: Building Practice (Days 22–45)** — "You're growing." Techniques expand to noting/labeling, loving-kindness, walking meditation, alternate nostril breathing, and Ujjayi breathing. Sessions build to **10–15 minutes**. Content introduces the science behind what's happening in users' brains and bodies. The mascot evolves — more confident, occasionally shown in different meditation positions.

**Phase 3: Deepening (Days 46–70)** — "Go deeper." Open awareness, visualization, mantra meditation, Kapalabhati, and breath retention enter the rotation. Sessions reach **15–20 minutes**. Content tackles emotional complexity, working with difficult feelings, and the "plateau" many meditators hit around Week 7. The mascot appears in more serene, expansive settings.

**Phase 4: Advanced Integration (Days 71–90)** — "Make it yours." Reflection meditation, non-dual awareness concepts, Wim Hof introduction, and building a personal practice toolkit. Sessions target **20+ minutes**. Content focuses on independence, long-term practice design, and integration into daily life. The mascot is shown confidently guiding others.

## Carousel design system and slide structure

Every carousel follows a consistent visual and structural template that maximizes swipe-through rate (the primary algorithm signal for carousel reach) and drives saves (the strongest quality signal in TikTok's algorithm).

### Recommended default structure: 7–8 slides

- **Slide 1 — The Hook**: Bold text headline (under 10 words) + purple mascot in an attention-grabbing pose or expression. This slide must create a curiosity gap or state a relatable problem. No logo. The mascot IS the brand signal.
- **Slide 2 — Context/Expansion**: Expand on the hook. State why this matters or set up the teaching. Keep under 25 words.
- **Slides 3–6 — Core Content**: One idea per slide, under 30 words each. For tutorials, one step per slide. For listicles, one item per slide. The mascot demonstrates or reacts to each point.
- **Slide 7 — Proof or Summary**: A science fact, stat, or summary that validates the teaching. Reinforces credibility.
- **Slide 8 — CTA**: "Save this for tonight 🧘" or "Try this before bed" + "Follow @still for Day [X+1]" + subtle app mention. Arrow pointing toward TikTok's save button.

### Technical specifications

All slides should be **1080 × 1920 pixels (9:16)** in PNG format. Maintain a consistent purple-dominant color palette with high-contrast white or light text. Left-align text. Keep text away from top 15% and bottom 20% of the frame (TikTok UI overlap zones). Use no more than **two font sizes** per slide — one for headers, one for body text. Captions should exceed **200 characters** and include 3–5 hashtags (#meditation #breathwork #mindfulness #mentalhealth #stillapp).

### Seven content pillar rotation

The 90 days rotate through these pillars to prevent repetitiveness while maintaining educational progression:

- **🧘 Technique Tutorial** (~30% of posts): Step-by-step how-tos for meditation and breathwork techniques
- **🧠 Science & Benefits** (~17% of posts): Research-backed explanations of why practices work
- **💡 Myth-Busting** (~13% of posts): Debunking misconceptions and reframing common objections
- **💪 Motivation & Mindset** (~13% of posts): Permission, encouragement, and perspective shifts
- **📊 Progress Check-in** (~7% of posts): Milestone markers, self-assessment, and celebration
- **⚡ Quick Tips & Hacks** (~10% of posts): Bite-sized, immediately actionable micro-practices
- **🎯 Daily Challenge** (~10% of posts): "Try this today" engagement-driving posts
