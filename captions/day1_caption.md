# Day 1 Caption — "Meditation Isn't What You Think It Is"

## TikTok (@stillmeditation)

Meditation isn't about clearing your mind. It never was.

Most people quit because they think they're "doing it wrong." But here's the truth — if you noticed your mind wandered, you're already meditating.

Think of it like reps at the gym. Every time your attention drifts and you bring it back? That's one rep. The wandering IS the workout.

You literally can't fail at this.

Save this if you've ever tried meditation and felt like you were doing it wrong. Follow @stillmeditation for Day 2: your first 3-minute meditation.
