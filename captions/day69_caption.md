# Day 69 Caption — "The two types of meditators at Day 70" (MOTIVATION)

## TikTok (@stillmeditation)

There are two kinds of meditators. One makes it to Day 70. One quietly disappears.

Type 1 chases the experience. They come to the cushion hunting for bliss states, deep flow, that transcendent feeling that makes meditation feel worth it. When a session delivers, they're hooked. When it doesn't, they wonder what they're doing wrong.

Type 2 shows up regardless. Good days and dull days look the same from the outside — they sit, they practice, they get up and get on with their life. No fireworks required.

Here is what happens to Type 1: ordinary sessions start to feel like failure. If nothing special arrived, the session didn't count. Over time, the ordinary sessions are most of them. So they stop coming.

Here is what Type 2 discovers: the ordinary sessions are the practice. Not a stepping stone to the peak experience — the peak experience is being fully present to this ordinary moment, right now, without needing it to be different.

The practice was never about achieving special states. It was always about learning to be fully here. Type 2 figures that out. Type 1 keeps waiting for it.

You are 69 days in. You already know which type you're becoming.
