# Day 70 Caption — "10 weeks: the deep practice self-assessment" (CHECK-IN)

## TikTok (@stillmeditation)

10 weeks of meditation. Let's see what's shifted.

This isn't a motivational post. It's an honest self-assessment — four questions that tell you exactly where your practice actually is. Not where you hope it is. Where it is.

At 10 weeks, the superficial changes are already visible. Sleep, focus, reactivity — those usually improve in the first four to six weeks. What you're measuring now is deeper. You're measuring whether the practice has become real. Whether it's moved from the cushion into your life.

Here are the four markers of a deep practice.

→ Can you sit 20 minutes without checking the clock? Not forcing it. Not white-knuckling through discomfort. Sitting with genuine ease, genuinely present. If 20 minutes feels natural, your nervous system has found a new baseline. That is a meaningful shift.

→ Can you label an emotion in real-time before it takes over? Not in retrospect, not in the therapy session two days later — but in the moment. You feel the tightening in your chest, you name it "fear" or "frustration," and there is a small gap between the feeling and your response. That gap is what ten weeks builds. Research from Harvard Medical School shows that labeling emotions reduces amygdala activation immediately. The label is the intervention.

→ Do you notice your breath during the day without trying? Walking to your car. In the middle of a conversation. Before you respond to an annoying email. If the breath shows up uninvited as an anchor, the practice has integrated. It is no longer a scheduled event. It is woven in.

→ Can you hold loving-kindness for a genuinely difficult person? Not a stranger. Not someone neutral. Someone who has actually wronged you or irritates you. If you can sit with that person in your mind and genuinely wish them peace without gritting your teeth — your compassion capacity has grown. That is the hardest one. That is also the most important one.

If you answered yes to most of these, you are not just consistent. You are ready for the final phase. Days 71 through 90 go deeper. The work gets quieter and more precise. The payoff compounds everything you've built.

If you're not there yet on one or two — that is information, not failure. It tells you exactly where to put your attention in the coming weeks.
