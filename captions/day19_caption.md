# Day 19 Caption — "Do's and Don'ts of Beginner Meditation" (MYTH)

## TikTok (@stillmeditation)

Stop doing these 5 things when you meditate.

Every beginner makes the same mistakes. Fix these and meditation gets dramatically easier — overnight.

**DON'T:**

→ Judge your sessions. There's no "good" or "bad" meditation. Showing up counts.
→ Force thoughts away. That's a struggle, not meditation. Let them pass.
→ Sit in physical pain. A chair is fine. A couch is fine. Comfort isn't cheating.
→ Wait for the right mood. The days you don't feel like it are the days it works hardest.
→ Compare to other people. Their 10 years of practice isn't a standard for your Day 19.

**DO:**

→ Show up daily. Even 2 minutes counts. Consistency beats duration.
→ Get comfortable. Back supported. Body relaxed. This isn't a test of endurance.
→ Start short. 5 minutes is a win. Build slowly.
→ Celebrate returning attention. Every time you notice you drifted and come back — that IS the rep.
→ Be patient with yourself. Your brain is rewiring. That takes weeks, not days.

Meditation isn't about being perfect. It's about returning. Again. And again. That IS the practice.
