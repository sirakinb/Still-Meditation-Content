# Day 57 Caption — "5 signs your meditation practice is working" (QUICK TIP)

## TikTok (@stillmeditation)

You will not notice the changes. The people around you will.

This is the strange thing about meditation. The shifts are quiet. They do not announce themselves. You will keep wondering if it is working — while everyone in your life slowly starts experiencing a different version of you.

Here are five signs the practice is doing its job, even when it does not feel like it.

→ You pause before reacting. The space between something happening and your response gets a little wider. You take a small breath where you used to fire off words. Half a second of quiet that changes the whole interaction.

→ You notice tension before it becomes pain. Tight shoulders, clenched jaw, shallow chest — you catch them earlier. Your body has a louder voice now, and you are finally listening to it before it has to scream.

→ You fall asleep faster. The looping thoughts at 11pm start to lose their grip. The mind that used to replay every awkward thing you said in 2014 finally lets you rest. Sleep gets easier without you doing anything to fix it.

→ You are more present in conversations. People feel heard when they talk to you. You are not rehearsing your reply. You are not checking your phone. You are actually there — and they can feel the difference even if they cannot name it.

→ A quiet confidence settles in. Not the loud kind. Not the swagger. The deep kind that comes from knowing you can handle whatever shows up. Less panic. Less spiraling. More steadiness.

Here is the part most people miss. None of this happens on the cushion. The cushion is the gym. Your life is where the workout shows up.

If you have been wondering whether your practice is working, stop checking how you feel during meditation. Start watching how you respond when something goes wrong.

That is the test. That is the proof.
