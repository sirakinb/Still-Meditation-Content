# Day 83 Caption — "I should be further along by now" (MYTH)

## TikTok (@stillmeditation)

There is no "further along" in meditation. There's only here.

That voice in your head that says you should be calmer by now, more consistent by now, more enlightened by now — that voice is not your meditation. That voice is your mind doing what it always does: measuring, comparing, creating a story about who you're supposed to be.

Comparison is just another thought. And noticing a thought is the practice.

Every session you've ever sat through — even the restless ones, the distracted ones, the ones where you couldn't stop thinking about your grocery list — was real practice. A scattered session at Day 83 teaches just as much as a quiet one at Day 3. The mind that wandered and returned a hundred times has been training the exact muscle meditation is designed to build.

Progress in meditation doesn't look like a straight line up. It looks like weather. Some days the mind is still. Other days it storms. Both are valid. Both are teaching you something. The session where you stayed with the storm and came back anyway? That was a breakthrough. You just couldn't see it yet.

Here's the reframe: the only metric in meditation is presence. Not calm. Not blank mind. Not how many days in a row you sat. Presence — the willingness to show up to this moment, exactly as it is.

You already have it. You're using it right now.
