# Day 4 Caption — "The 1-Minute Breathing Challenge"

## TikTok (@stillmeditation)

You have 60 seconds. That's all this takes.

4 counts in. 4 counts out. Repeat until the timer goes off.

Notice how you feel after. That shift is your nervous system calming down — in under a minute.

Save this for the next time you feel stressed, anxious, or overwhelmed.
