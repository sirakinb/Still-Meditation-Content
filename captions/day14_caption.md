# Day 14 Caption — "Week 2 Check-in: What You Should Be Noticing" (CHECK-IN)

## TikTok (@stillmeditation)

If you've been practicing for 2 weeks, read this.

Meditation still feels hard. That is completely normal. Two weeks in and you probably feel like you're doing it wrong. You're not.

Here's what real progress actually looks like:

→ You're sleeping a little better. Maybe not every night — but a few nights where you fell asleep faster or woke up more rested. That's your nervous system recalibrating.

→ You catch yourself lost in thought. You zone out, then notice you zoned out. That moment of noticing IS meditation working.

→ You notice tension you didn't know you carried. Tight jaw. Clenched fists. Shoulders near your ears. You're not more tense — you're more aware.

→ You pause before reacting. Even once. Even for half a second. That tiny gap between stimulus and response is your prefrontal cortex strengthening.

All of this is real progress. Even if your mind still wanders. You are rewiring your brain. Keep going — Week 3 builds on everything you've done.
