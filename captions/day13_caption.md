# Day 13 Caption — "Coherent Breathing: The Most Research-Backed Technique" (TUTORIAL)

## TikTok (@stillmeditation)

Navy SEALs use it before missions. Neuroscientists study it more than any other technique. It's called coherent breathing.

Here's how to do it:
→ Inhale for 5 seconds (through your nose, belly first)
→ Exhale for 5 seconds (nose or mouth, let everything go)
→ That's one cycle. Repeat for 5 minutes.

6 breaths per minute. That's the magic number.

This specific rhythm maximizes heart rate variability — the #1 indicator of stress resilience. It literally syncs your heart, lungs, and nervous system.

5 minutes a day. 30 breath cycles. Your baseline stress drops within 2 weeks.
