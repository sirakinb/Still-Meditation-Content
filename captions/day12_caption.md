# Day 12 Caption — "I Don't Have Time to Meditate" (MYTH)

## TikTok (@stillmeditation)

You scrolled to this post. You have time to meditate.

The biggest myth about meditation: that you need 30 minutes, a quiet room, and perfect silence. None of that is true.

Here's what the research actually says:
→ Just 5–10 minutes of daily meditation produces measurable changes in stress, focus, and emotional regulation in 8 weeks
→ 5 minutes × 6 days a week beats 30 minutes once a week
→ Your brain rewires through repetition, not marathon sessions

You brushed your teeth today. That took 2 minutes and you didn't think about it. Meditation can be that simple.

5 minutes you already have:
• Before you check your phone in the morning
• Waiting for your coffee to brew
• Right before bed

Pick one. Stop waiting for the perfect moment. It will not come.

5 minutes today is more powerful than 30 minutes someday. Save this and start tomorrow.
