# Day 20 Caption — "4-7-8 Breathing: Fall Asleep in Minutes" (TUTORIAL)

## TikTok (@stillmeditation)

Can't sleep? Try the military's secret breathing method.

It's called 4-7-8 breathing. Developed by Dr. Andrew Weil. Used by the military to fall asleep in under 2 minutes on demand.

Here's how to do it:

→ Step 1: Inhale through your nose for 4 counts. Slow and quiet. Fill your belly, not your chest.
→ Step 2: Hold your breath for 7 counts. Keep your body relaxed. Don't tense up. This is where the magic starts.
→ Step 3: Exhale through your mouth for 8 counts. Long, slow, and audible. Let every last bit of air out.

That's one round. Do 4 rounds total. Takes about 1 minute.

Why it works: the long exhale activates your parasympathetic nervous system — the branch responsible for "rest and digest." Your heart rate drops. Your muscles release. Your brain gets the signal that it's safe to power down.

Most people feel the shift by round 2. By round 4, you're drowsy. If you're still wired after 4 rounds, do 4 more. It compounds.

One important note: don't use this if you're driving or need to stay alert. It's that effective.
