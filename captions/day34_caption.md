# Day 34 Caption — "Your first 10-minute practice (with a twist)" (TUTORIAL)

## TikTok (@stillmeditation)

Most beginners pick one technique and grind it for weeks.

There is a better way to start. Combine the three core trainings into a single 10-minute sit. You touch focus, awareness, and warmth all in one session. Your brain learns faster because it gets to feel how the skills connect.

Here is the structure.

→ Minutes 1 to 3. Breath awareness. Sit comfortably. Eyes soft or closed. Feel the inhale. Feel the exhale. When the mind wanders, bring it back. This is the focus rep.

→ Minutes 4 to 6. Body scan. Drop your attention into the top of your head. Move it slowly down. Forehead. Jaw. Shoulders. Chest. Belly. Hips. Legs. Feet. Notice without fixing. This is the awareness rep.

→ Minutes 7 to 9. Loving-kindness. Place a hand on your heart. Repeat silently. May I be safe. May I be healthy. May I be at peace. Then send the same wishes to someone you love. Then to someone neutral. This is the warmth rep.

→ Minute 10. Open sitting. Drop every technique. Just sit. Notice whatever arises. Sound. Sensation. Thought. Let it come and go.

That is it. Ten minutes. Three trainings. One sit.

This is how experienced meditators actually structure their sessions. You are not stuck doing one thing forever. You are layering skills.

Set a timer. Try it once today.
