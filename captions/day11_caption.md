# Day 11 Caption — "5-Minute Body Scan Before Bed"

## TikTok (@stillmeditation)

Tonight, try this instead of scrolling.

A 5-minute body scan before bed helps you fall asleep faster, release tension you didn't know you were holding, and wake up more rested.

Here's how:
1. Lie down, close your eyes
2. Start at the top of your head — 30 seconds
3. Neck & shoulders — 30 sec
4. Arms & hands — 30 sec
5. Chest & belly — 30 sec
6. Hips — 30 sec
7. Legs — 30 sec
8. Feet — 30 sec

Then let your awareness expand to your whole body. Breathe. You're done.

The rule: notice tension without trying to fix it. That's the whole practice.
