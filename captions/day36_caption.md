# Day 36 Caption — "Loving-kindness level 2: the expanding circle" (TUTORIAL)

## TikTok (@stillmeditation)

You already know self-compassion. Now stretch the circle.

Loving-kindness level 2 is a 10-minute practice that walks your heart through five concentric circles. Each one is harder than the last. Each one stretches your capacity for compassion a little further.

Here is the full sequence.

→ Minutes 1–2: Yourself. Hand on chest. May I be safe. May I be happy. May I be at peace. This is the foundation. You cannot pour from an empty cup.

→ Minutes 3–4: A loved one. Picture someone easy to love — a child, a partner, a close friend. Hold their face in your mind. Send them the same wishes. Notice how your chest opens.

→ Minutes 5–6: A neutral person. The barista. A coworker you barely know. The neighbor down the hall. Someone you have no strong feelings about. Wish them peace. This is where it stops being automatic and starts being practice.

→ Minutes 7–8: A difficult person. Not your worst enemy. Just someone who frustrates you. The colleague who interrupts. The relative who pushes your buttons. Wish them well. This is the hardest circle. You will resist it. Stay with it.

→ Minutes 9–10: All beings everywhere. Open the circle wide. Every person you have ever met. Every person you will never meet. May all beings be free from suffering. This is where compassion goes from personal to universal.

The science: this practice activates the same brain regions as parenting and bonding. Eight weeks of consistent loving-kindness measurably increases positive emotion and social connection. It is not soft. It is structural training for the heart.

Do it tonight. 10 minutes. Five circles. Watch what loosens.
