# Day 7 Caption — "Why Your Mind Won't Shut Up (And Why That's Good)"

## TikTok (@stillmeditation)

The #1 reason people quit meditation: "my mind won't stop."

But here's what nobody tells you — that's not a problem. That's just a brain doing its job. The average person has 6,000+ thoughts a day. Every single one of them.

Meditation isn't about stopping the thoughts. It's about what you do when you notice them.

And every time you notice and come back? That's one mental push-up. A busier mind just means more reps.

Save this for the next time you think you're bad at meditating. You're not. You're just getting more reps.
