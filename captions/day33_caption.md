# Day 33 Caption — "You're not meditating wrong — you're building something" (MOTIVATION)

## TikTok (@stillmeditation)

You sit down to meditate.

Your mind wanders. You get restless. You wonder if you are doing this right at all.

Here is what nobody told you. The wandering is not the failure. Noticing it is the rep.

Meditation is not arrival. It is training. Every time your attention drifts and you bring it back, you just did one bicep curl for the part of your brain that decides what you focus on. That is the work.

You spent the first month of this practice quietly building one thing — the attention muscle. The part of you that can stay with one thing instead of being yanked around by every thought, ping, and craving.

Now you are in month two. The muscle you built is learning new skills. Loving-kindness. Compassion. The ability to sit with feelings instead of running from them. The expansion from seeing clearly to feeling deeply.

→ Month 1 was focus.

→ Month 2 is feeling.

Both are meditation. Both are you growing.

The doubt you feel right now — the "is this working" moment — is the exact moment most people quit. It is also the moment the practice starts to actually change you. Stay.

You are not meditating wrong. You are building something. Keep showing up.

You are already there.
