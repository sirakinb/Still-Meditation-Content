# Day 15 Caption — "The labeling trick that makes meditation click" (TUTORIAL)

## TikTok (@stillmeditation)

This one word changes everything about meditation 🧠

Most people think meditation means clearing your mind. So when a thought pops up, they fight it. They get frustrated. They give up.

But there's a simpler way — and it only takes one word.

It's called noting (or labeling). When a thought appears, you silently whisper: "thinking." When an emotion rises up: "feeling." When a sound distracts you: "hearing." Then gently return to your breath.

That one quiet label creates distance between you and your mental noise. You're no longer inside the thought — you're watching it. You become the observer, not the observed. That shift is everything.

→ Notice a thought arise
→ Whisper its label: thinking, feeling, or hearing
→ Return to your breath. No judgment. Just return.
